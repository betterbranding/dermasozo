Brand Guidelines

Clinical Skin Recovery · Est. 2026

01 · Logo System

Two lockups, one brand

The stacked crest leads on products and packaging; the horizontal lockup leads on the website, letterheads, and documents. Never redraw, re-color, or re-typeset either mark.

Primary — Products & Packaging

Stacked Crest

Teal ring crest with the Regeneration Burst — radiant teal & coral cilia around a coral core — above the wordmark — coral derma, teal Sozo script. Use on labels, cartons, and social avatars.

Primary — Web & Letterhead

Horizontal Lockup

Crest at left of the wordmark for headers, email signatures, letterheads, and any wide format.

Reversed — Dark Grounds

Reversed Stacked

For navy or teal backgrounds. Maintain contrast; never place the standard mark on dark grounds.

Rules

Usage

✓Clearspace = height of the burst crest on all sides
✓Minimum size: 32 px crest height (stacked), 140 px width (horizontal)
✕No stretching, recoloring, drop shadows, or outlines
✕No taglines or icons locked to the mark
✕“SoZo” casing is sacred — never “Sozo” or “SOZO” in text

05 · In the World

Product photography

Cobalt-blue bottle, white sprayer top, blush or marble surfaces, soft daylight, soft coral accents. Spa-clinical — never clinical-cold.

06 · Voice

How DermaSoZo speaks

The customer is the hero

StoryBrand framing: she wants her skin back — DermaSoZo is the calm, clinical guide with a proven 3-step plan. We never make the brand the hero.

CAFE cadence

Captivate with the moment of damage, agitate the cost of waiting, fascinate with the recovery story, educate with the clinical why.

Tone

Warm authority. Short sentences. Evidence over hype. “Recover Beautifully.” is the promise — clinical results, beautifully delivered.

Vocabulary

Say: recovery, clinical-grade, protocol, barrier. Avoid: miracle, cure, anti-aging clichés, fear-based burn language on-pack.
